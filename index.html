3. How Data Is Collected

  • Data provided directly by users (e.g., creating an account, adding transactions).
  • Data generated through app usage (e.g., logs).
  • Permissions: Camera and storage access are accessed only with explicit user permission.

4. Purpose of Data Usage

  • OTP-based authentication and account security.
  • Account creation and management.
  • Statement scanning and processing.
  • Transaction and budget tracking.
  • Providing insights and summaries of your finances.
  • App security and fraud prevention.
  • Customer support.

5. App Permissions Explanation

  • Camera: Required for scanning physical bank statements or receipts.
  • Storage: Required to upload or select statement files (PDF/Images) from your device.
  • Internet: Required to sync data and store it securely on our backend.
  • Notifications: Used for reminders and alerts (if enabled).

6. Data Storage & Processing

  • Data is stored in a secure backend database.
  • All data transmission is secured using HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • We implement reasonable security safeguards to protect your data.
  • Note: While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your privacy and adhere to strict data sharing principles:

  • No Selling: We do not sell your personal or financial data.
  • No Advertising: We do not use your data for advertising purposes.
  • No Analytics SDKs: We do not use third-party analytics trackers.

Data is shared only:

  • If legally required by law.
  • With essential service providers (e.g., cloud hosting) under strict confidentiality agreements.

8. Data Retention

  • Data is retained only while your account is active.
  • Financial and personal data is linked to your user account.
  • Retention ends immediately upon account deletion.

9. User Control & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete:

  • Individual transactions
  • Uploaded statements
  • Your entire account

Account Deletion: Deleting your account permanently removes all your data from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

If you need assistance with deletion, please contact our support team.
